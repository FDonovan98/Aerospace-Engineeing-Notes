	\subsection{Power}
		Power is the energy dissipated per second, usually in the form of heat. The unit Watts (W) is used. The power , P, dissipated in a resistor, R, is given by
		\begin{align*}
			P&=IV
			\intertext{Using Ohm's law}
			P&=I^2R\\
			P&=\frac{V^2}{R}
		\end{align*}
